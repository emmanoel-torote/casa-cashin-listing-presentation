Location & Walkability

A rare combination of elevation, ocean views, and walk-to-everything convenience in Las Tunas

Casa Cashin sits across the street from Baja Temple, one of Las Tunas' most recognized boutique hotels (4.7★, 113 reviews), within walking distance of the corridor's best dining and leisure destinations. This level of walkability is genuinely scarce in the Todos Santos market — most comparables require a car for everything.

The property sits on a natural rise within Las Tunas, providing partial Pacific views from the living room — a genuine rarity in the area and a key driver of vacation rental ADR.

Being across from a highly-rated boutique hotel validates the neighborhood and creates organic guest discovery — potential renters already know where Baja Temple is.

Two-Level Lot Opportunity

The 1,500m² lot has a lower street-level section and a raised section where the home is sited. Significant room to expand — a rare option in established Las Tunas.
